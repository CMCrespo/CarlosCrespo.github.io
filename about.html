A Little About Me

I'm not your typical Geographer. During the day, I oversee law enforcement and port security operations for the U.S. Coast Guard in the Mid-Atlantic Region and Delaware River. I also manage maritime Search and Rescue missions. At night, I don my superhero costume and hone my map making and coding skills studying cartography and web-design at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Go Badgers!

My wife and I, along with our two boys, enjoy traveling, camping, sports, and music.
